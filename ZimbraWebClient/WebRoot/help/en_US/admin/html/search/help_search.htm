Zimbra Search

Zimbra search is a powerful unified search to quickly find answers to common questions. When you enter a search query in the search field and click Search Zimbra, Zimbra's wiki, forums, our bug tracking database, and documents are searched. The results are displayed in a new window with links to the information.

What is searched

The results displayed are based on whether ZCSis the network edition or the open source edition.

  • Zimbra Wiki - Articles developed by Zimbra support and the community that covers common problems and procedures. Articles that are marked as certified have been verified by Zimbra as being correct information.

  • Zimbra Forums - Zimbra community share technical information of a general nature here. Users ask questions about ZCS and the community responds.

  • Zimbra Documentation - All published Zimbra official documentation posted to the Zimbra website is searched

  • Bug tracking database - Zimbra's bug tracking tool, Bugzilla, is searched.
