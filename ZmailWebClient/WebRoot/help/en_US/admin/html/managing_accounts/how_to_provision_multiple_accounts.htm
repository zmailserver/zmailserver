How to Provision Multiple Accounts

Before you start using the Bulk Provisioning Accounts wizard, you must have the account information saved in a .csv file.  See Bulk Provisioning for information about creating the .csv file.

  1. Click Bulk Provisioning to open the Bulk Provisioning Accounts Wizard.

  2. In the Upload the CSV file dialog, browse to the .csv to upload. Click Next.  

The Provision Accounts dialog displays account information that is in the .csv file. Accounts that were not assigned passwords are automatically assigned a randomly generated password. When the password is automatically generated, users are required to change it when they first log on.

    • If any errors are displayed, click Cancel. You must fix the .csv files before you can proceed.

    1. Click Next to provision the accounts. The Provisioning accounts dialog shows the progress as the accounts are created.

    2. When all accounts are provisioned, click OK.  A Summary dialog lists the account information again.  You can click Download CSV report to save a copy of this information to your computer.

    3. Click Finish. The accounts are listed in the Accounts content page.
