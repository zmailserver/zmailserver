Using Zimlets

Zimlets are add-on applications that enhance the functionality of your client. When a Zimlet is added to @ClientNameShort@, you can look at information and interact with the third-party application from within your email messages.

Several pre-configured, default Zimlets are included when @ClientNameShort@ is installed. You can enable optional Zimlets from the Zimlet page, add Zimlets from the Zimbra Gallery, or create customized Zimlets so that you can interact with your company resources or other defined applications from the @ClientNameLong@.
