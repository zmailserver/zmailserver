Managing Your Zimlets

Zimlets are add-on applications that enhance the functionality of your client. When a Zimlet is added to ZWC, you can look at information and interact with the third-party application from within your email messages.

Several pre-configured, default Zimlets are included when @ClientNameShort@ is installed.  

There are Zimlets that are included in the @ClientNameLong@ and considered default Zimlets.

Procedure

  1. Go to Preferences>Zimlets page.

  2. Select the Zimlets you want to enable.

  3. Click Save.
